Helga Hufflepuff

Helga Hufflepuff is one of the four founders of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the Harry Potter series. She is known for her values of hard work, loyalty, and fair play. Hufflepuff House is characterized by its inclusivity and dedication to nurturing students of all backgrounds. Helga is often depicted as kind-hearted and dedicated to her students, emphasizing that everyone deserves a chance to succeed.
