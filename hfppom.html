Pomona Sprout

Pomona Sprout is a witch and the head of Hufflepuff House at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. She is also the professor of Herbology, where she teaches students about magical plants and their properties. Known for her nurturing demeanor, she is deeply passionate about plants and cares for her students with a motherly approach. Pomona is depicted as knowledgeable, wise, and practical, with a strong sense of loyalty to her house and the students she teaches. During the events of the series, she plays a role in the fight against Voldemort and his followers, demonstrating her bravery and commitment to the fight for good.
