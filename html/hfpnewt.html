Newt Scamander

Newt Scamander is a Magizoologist and the protagonist of the "Fantastic Beasts" film series. He is known for his deep love and understanding of magical creatures, which is reflected in his famous book, "Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them." Newt is a Hufflepuff, which highlights his traits of loyalty, hard work, and compassion, particularly toward creatures that others may overlook or fear. He possesses a unique brand of magic focused on care and protection, often using it to create a safe haven for various magical creatures in his enchanted suitcase. His character embodies curiosity and empathy, making him a pivotal figure in the wizarding world.
